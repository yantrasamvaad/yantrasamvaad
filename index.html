About this training

YantraSamvaad's OpenFOAM training is designed with an academic rigor and practical orientation. Participants learn how to select solvers, prepare meshes, implement boundary conditions, run transient and steady simulations, and validate results through systematic workflows.

Learning outcomes

  • Understand OpenFOAM architecture and common solvers
  • Set up CFD cases from geometry to post-processing
  • Apply turbulence modelling and mesh refinement strategies
  • Develop validation and verification approaches

Course modules (Representative - can be tweaked as per user requirements)

Beginner — Foundations (Track A)

Introduction to CFD concepts and OpenFOAM fundamentals. Ideal for participants new to CFD or new to OpenFOAM.

  • OpenFOAM installation & environment
  • Case structure: blockMesh, boundary conditions
  • Simple solvers: Single Phase Flows, Simple Heat Transfer etc.
  • Post-processing with ParaView

Intermediate — Practical Workflows (Track B)

Hands-on workflows for common engineering problems and improved result reliability.

  • Meshing strategies and refinement-snappyHexMesh
  • Turbulence models and solver choices
  • Transient simulations and stability
  • Conjugate heat transfer and multiphase flows

Advanced — Customisation & Optimization (Track C)

Advanced topics for research and production workflows, including solver customization and HPC considerations.

  • Custom boundary conditions and function objects
  • Parallel runs and performance tuning
  • Solver development and debugging
  • Programming Libraries from Scratch
